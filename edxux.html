ONGOING

Project Overview

An ongoing project for my edX MicroMasters

The Challenge

Over-reporting or estimating physical and/or mental fitness is a well-documented problem - people tend to overestimate how often they "regularly" go to the gym, eat healthy, etc. I'm designing a journalling app that helps people reflect on their day, which displays as quantified data in their provider's portal. The goal is to simultaneously improve health mindfulness in patient users and give health providers access to more accurate, quantified data on their patients' health habits.

Research

I started the project off by doing preliminary market research. Although the health & wellness app market is over-saturated, most apps have a narrow focus (i.e. weight loss) rather than a holistic scope. In the age of self-quanitification, few apps provide any meaningful way for patients &s; providers to use the data.

User Interviews have been conducted with users currently tracking some aspect of wellness (steps, sleep, mood). Further research is needed as the prototype is developed.
