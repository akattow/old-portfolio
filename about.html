About Kat

My Work

I'm a left-brain/right-brain hybrid type and a self-taught user experience designer with a professional background in visual and graphic design. My experience also includes small business and non-profit marketing - bonus!

The Crash

In April 2016, while working in graphic design and marketing, I crashed my moped on a pothole. I was extremely lucky to emerge from the ER without any permanent damage. I had a fractured C7 vertebrae, scapula, and two fractured teeth.

The hospital and the subsequent months of specialists, oral surgery, insurance questions, and slow healing was a revelation. I truly understood that design is "not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."

I'm passionate about the potential to improve not only healthcare and patient experience, but all aspects of life when people need help the most. Since 2016, I've been studying UX Research & Design and learned front-end development.

What I Do

I'm most passionate about visual storytelling, user-centered design, and trying to make the world a slightly better place. When I'm not working on my JavaScript skills, you'll find me at the gym training for my next powerlifting meet or at home doodling cartoon pugs.
